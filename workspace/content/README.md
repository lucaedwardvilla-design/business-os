# Content

Your content library - everything you create for marketing and sales.

---

## Suggested Structure

```
content/
├── youtube/          # Video scripts, ideas, outlines
├── newsletter/       # Email content
├── social/           # Social media posts
├── blog/             # Long-form articles
└── assets/           # Images, templates, etc.
```

---

## How to Use

When you create content with the AI, save it here organized by type.

Example workflow:
1. "Help me outline a YouTube video about X"
2. AI creates outline
3. Save to `content/youtube/video-title.md`

---

## Content Planning

You might also add:
- `content/calendar.md` - Publication schedule
- `content/ideas.md` - Content ideas backlog

---

## Get Started

Create folders as you need them. Don't overthink the structure - it can evolve.
