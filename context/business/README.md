# Business Context

This folder contains everything the AI needs to know about your business.

---

## What to Add Here

### company.md
Describe your business:
- What you do
- Who you serve
- Your mission/purpose
- Company history (brief)

### offers.md
Your products and services:
- What you sell
- Pricing (if relevant)
- Delivery process
- What makes you different

### clients.md
Your ideal customer:
- Who they are
- What problems they have
- What they want to achieve
- How you help them

### voice.md
Your brand voice:
- Tone (professional, casual, etc.)
- Words you use/avoid
- Example sentences
- Personality traits

---

## Why This Matters

The more context here, the less you explain to the AI.

Without context: "Write me an email to a prospect"
With context: "Write me an email to a prospect" → AI knows your services, ideal client, and voice

---

## Example: company.md

```markdown
# Company Overview

We help small business owners automate their operations using AI tools.

**Services:**
- AI automation consulting
- Custom workflow design
- Implementation support

**Founded:** 2024
**Location:** Remote
**Team size:** Solo founder
```

---

## Get Started

Create these files one at a time. Start with `company.md` - even a few sentences help.
